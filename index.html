About us

DuKu Business is a leading Indian multinational conglomerate that has consistently dominated the business landscape since its inception in 2017. Under the visionary leadership of Dev Upreti, also known as Jimmy, we have grown into a global powerhouse, managing a diverse portfolio of over 200 companies across various industries.

As a forward-thinking company, DuKu Business specializes in a wide array of internet services and cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions, delivering innovative products and services to businesses and consumers around the world. Our commitment to excellence and continuous innovation has positioned us as an industry leader, always ahead of the curve in technology and market trends.

From digital infrastructure and e-commerce solutions to AI-powered services that reshape industries, DuKu Business is driven by a passion for technology and a mission to empower businesses globally. With a dedicated team of professionals and a forward-looking approach, we continue to grow and redefine the future of business on a global scale.
At DuKu Business, we don’t just stay ahead; we shape the future. Join us as we continue to lead, innovate, and inspire across industries worldwide.
